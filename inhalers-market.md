<p><p>The inhalers market comprises various types designed for respiratory therapy. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) deliver a specific dose of medication through a pressurized canister, providing rapid relief. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) dispense powdered medication activated by inhalation, offering an easy and breath-activated delivery method. Nasal inhalers are designed for intranasal delivery, targeting allergies or congestion. Other inhalers encompass devices like nebulizers, which convert liquid medication into a mist, and soft mist inhalers that create a slow-moving aerosol for effective delivery.</p></p>
