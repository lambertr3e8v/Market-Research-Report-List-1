<p><p>The Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) systems market is primarily categorized into two types of membranes: polyaromatic polymers membranes and partially fluorinated polymers membranes. Polyaromatic polymers membranes are known for their thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, partially fluorinated polymers membranes offer improved chemical resistance and enhanced ionic conductivity, which are crucial for efficient fuel cell operation. Both types cater to diverse applications in transportation, stationary power, and portable energy solutions.</p></p>
