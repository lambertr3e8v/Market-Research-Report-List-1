<p><ul><li>Esophageal Stent</li><li>Duodenal Stent</li><li>Colonic Stent</li><li>Biliary Stent</li><li>Pancreatic Stent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The upper gastrointestinal stent market comprises various types designed to address specific organ-related conditions. Esophageal stents aid in treating esophageal strictures or cancers, facilitating swallowing. Duodenal stents help patients with obstructions in the duodenum, improving digestion. Colonic stents are used for large bowel obstructions, promoting bowel function. Biliary stents relieve bile duct obstructions, ensuring proper bile flow. Lastly, pancreatic stents assist in treating pancreatic duct obstructions, thereby improving drainage and reducing complications. Each stent type plays a crucial role in enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
