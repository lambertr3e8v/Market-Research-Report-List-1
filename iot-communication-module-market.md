<p><ul><li>Cellular Communication Module</li><li>Non-cellular Communication Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT communication module market is divided into two main types: cellular and non-cellular communication modules. Cellular communication modules use mobile networks (like 4G or 5G) to facilitate data transmission, offering broad coverage and reliability for applications requiring constant connectivity. In contrast, non-cellular communication modules utilize technologies such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, or Zigbee, which are ideal for short-range communication and lower power consumption. Both types cater to diverse applications, from smart cities to industrial automation, based on connectivity needs.</p></p>
