<p><strong>The Lumbar Disc Replacement Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-on-metal</li><li>Metal-on-biopolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lumbar disc replacement devices are categorized into two primary types: metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer. Metal-on-metal devices consist of metallic components that provide durability and strength, ideal for patients requiring robust support. In contrast, metal-on-biopolymer devices incorporate a combination of metal components and biopolymer materials, promoting a more natural range of motion and compatibility with biological tissues. Both types aim to alleviate pain and restore function in patients suffering from degenerative disc disease while minimizing the risks associated with traditional spinal fusion treatments.</p></p>
