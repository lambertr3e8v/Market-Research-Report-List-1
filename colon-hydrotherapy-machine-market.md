<p><strong>The Colon Hydrotherapy Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open System</li><li>Close System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colon hydrotherapy machine market is primarily divided into two types: open systems and closed systems. Open systems allow patients to expel waste into a basin while water is introduced, promoting comfort and user control. In contrast, closed systems utilize a sealed mechanism where waste is removed through a closed tube, offering more privacy and cleanliness. Both systems aim to provide detoxification benefits, but their design and operation cater to different preferences and comfort levels of users during treatment.</p></p>
