<p><p>The Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) hardware market comprises several key components. Magnets generate the strong magnetic fields necessary for NMR. Field locks maintain magnetic stability, ensuring accuracy in measurements. Shim coils optimize field homogeneity. Sample probes hold the samples and facilitate signal detection. RF coils transmit and receive radiofrequency signals, essential for data acquisition. Gradient coils create spatial variations in the magnetic field, enhancing resolution. Quadrature detectors improve signal detection by capturing multiple signal components for more accurate analysis.</p></p>
