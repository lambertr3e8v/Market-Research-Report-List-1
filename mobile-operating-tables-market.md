<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile operating tables market encompasses various types, including manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic tables. Manual tables rely on mechanical adjustments for positioning, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Electric tables utilize motorized systems for easy height and angle adjustments, enhancing convenience. Hydraulic tables use fluid power for smooth and precise positioning, favored in surgical settings for stability. Electro-hydraulic tables combine electrical controls with hydraulic systems, providing versatility and precise adjustments, making them popular in modern operating rooms.</p></p>
