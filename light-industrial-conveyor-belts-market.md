<p><p>The light industrial conveyor belts market comprises various types based on manufacturing processes, primarily the coating and calendering processes. The coating process involves applying a layer of material onto a fabric base to enhance durability and resistance, making belts suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, the calendering process utilizes heat and pressure to produce thin, uniform sheets of rubber or polymer that are then transformed into belts. Both processes result in belts optimized for specific industrial needs, balancing flexibility with performance.</p></p>
