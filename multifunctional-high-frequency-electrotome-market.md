<p><p>The Multifunctional High Frequency Electrotome market is primarily segmented into two circuit types: monopolar and bipolar. The monopolar circuit involves a single active electrode that generates high-frequency current, allowing for deeper tissue penetration and precision in cutting and coagulation. In contrast, the bipolar circuit uses two electrodes, facilitating more controlled tissue effects with minimal thermal spread, making it ideal for delicate procedures. Both circuit types cater to diverse surgical needs in various medical specialties, enhancing operational efficiency and patient outcomes.</p></p>
