<p><p>The LEO (Low Earth Orbit) antenna market is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active LEO antennas are equipped with integrated electronic systems that enhance signal transmission and reception, allowing for dynamic communication with satellites. In contrast, passive LEO antennas rely on simple designs without electronic components, primarily reflecting or refracting signals. Both types serve distinct applications, with active antennas offering more functionality and flexibility, while passive antennas are often used in cost-sensitive environments.</p></p>
