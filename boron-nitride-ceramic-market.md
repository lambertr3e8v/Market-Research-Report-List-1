<p><p>The Boron Nitride Ceramic Market is primarily divided into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I includes hexagonal boron nitride (h-BN), known for its lubricating properties and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Type II encompasses cubic boron nitride (c-BN), which is renowned for its hardness, often used in cutting tools and abrasive materials. These distinctions allow for specialized applications across industries such as manufacturing, electronics, and aerospace, catering to diverse performance needs.</p></p>
