<p><strong>The Facade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Ventilated Façade</li><li>Ventilated Façade</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facade market types can be categorized into Non-Ventilated Façades, Ventilated Façades, and Others. Non-Ventilated Façades consist of solid wall systems that do not allow airflow, focusing on aesthetic appeal and insulation. Ventilated Façades incorporate an air gap between the outer cladding and the building, promoting airflow and improved thermal regulation. The "Others" category includes innovative or less common façade systems, such as green walls or integrated solar technology, catering to niche markets and diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
