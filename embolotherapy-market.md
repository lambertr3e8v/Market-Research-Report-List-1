<p><p>The embolotherapy market encompasses various types of products used to obstruct blood flow in targeted areas. Embolic agents can include microspheres, which are small particles that promote clotting, and embolic coils, metallic devices that occlude blood vessels. Liquid embolic agents, such as glue-like substances, are utilized for vascular occlusion. Embolic plug systems and detachable balloons offer innovative methods for blocking blood flow. Additionally, essential support devices, microcatheters, and guidewires facilitate the efficient delivery of these embolic agents during procedures.</p></p>
