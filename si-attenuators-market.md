<p><p>Si attenuators in the market are categorized mainly into Super High Frequency (SHF) and Ultra High Frequency (UHF) types. SHF attenuators operate within the 3 to 30 gigahertz range, making them ideal for applications like radar and satellite communications, where bandwidth and precise signal control are crucial. UHF attenuators, on the other hand, function from 300 megahertz to 3 gigahertz, serving areas such as television broadcasting and two-way radio communication. These segments cater to diverse technological needs across various industries.</p></p>
