<p><strong>The Fiber-Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-optic sensors market is divided into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors use the fiber itself as the sensing element, detecting changes in light properties caused by environmental factors like temperature or strain. In contrast, extrinsic fiber-optic sensors employ external components, such as transducers, to measure physical phenomena and convert them into light signals. Both types offer high sensitivity and are widely used in various applications, including structural health monitoring, environmental sensing, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
