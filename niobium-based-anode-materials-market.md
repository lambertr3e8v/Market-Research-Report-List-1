<p><p>The niobium-based anode materials market primarily consists of two types: anode sheets and anode powder. Anode powder, on the other hand, consists of finely ground niobium particles, which are advantageous for applications requiring high surface area and electrochemical performance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy storage and efficiency in various technologies, including lithium-ion and solid-state batteries.</p></p>
