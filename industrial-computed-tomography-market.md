<p><p>The Industrial Computed Tomography market is categorized into three main types based on energy levels and focus. High Energy Industrial CT utilizes higher radiation energies to analyze dense materials, ideal for complex assemblies in aerospace and automotive sectors. Low Energy Industrial CT employs lower energies for inspecting less dense materials, often in the electronics and plastic industries. Mini-Focus Industrial CT features a smaller focal spot, providing high-resolution images for detailed examination of intricate components, widely used in quality control processes.</p></p>
