<p><strong>The Nanometer Titania Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rutile</li><li>Anatase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanometer titania market is primarily divided into two types: rutile and anatase. Rutile nanometer titania is characterized by its high stability, superior UV absorption capabilities, and excellent photocatalytic properties, making it suitable for applications in coatings, plastics, and sunscreens. Anatase, on the other hand, is valued for its superior photocatalytic activity and lower density, often used in environmental applications like wastewater treatment and air purification. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driven by their unique properties and functionalities.</p></p>
