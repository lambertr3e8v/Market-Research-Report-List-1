<p><ul><li>Half Floating Axle/Semi Floating Axle</li><li>Three Quarter Floating Axle</li><li>Fully Floating Rear Axle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive axles market is categorized into various types based on their design and function. Half floating and semi floating axles support both the vehicle's weight and the drive torque, with semi floating designs typically featuring a single wheel hub. Three quarter floating axles offer enhanced load distribution, allowing for better performance under heavy loads. Fully floating rear axles have independent wheel support, improving durability and making them ideal for heavy-duty applications, providing enhanced handling and reliability.</p></p>
