<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection Market is divided into two main types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. </p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging involves detecting naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, allowing for inspection without external energy sources. It is ideal for thermal imaging and non-destructive testing. </p><p>In contrast, Active Terahertz Imaging employs external terahertz sources to illuminate the target, enhancing imaging capabilities. This method is effective for applications requiring greater sensitivity and control, such as material characterization and security inspections.</p></p>
