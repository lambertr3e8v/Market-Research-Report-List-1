<p><p>The fluorescent pigments market is categorized primarily into thermoset and thermoplastic types, along with other formulations like aqueous dispersions. Thermoset pigments, which cure into a permanent structure upon heat application, are favored for applications requiring durability. Thermoplastic pigments, on the other hand, remain malleable upon heating and are often used in flexible applications. Additionally, aqueous dispersions provide eco-friendly options with water-based formulations, catering to industries seeking lower environmental impact while maintaining vibrant color and stability.</p></p>
