<p><p>Gel-filled fiber cables are designed to provide enhanced protection and performance in various networking applications. The market is divided into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode fibers are optimized for long-distance communication, allowing light to travel straight down the core, minimizing signal loss. In contrast, Multi-Mode fibers support multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances with higher data bandwidth. Both types leverage gel filling to prevent moisture intrusion and enhance durability, ensuring reliable connectivity in diverse environments.</p></p>
