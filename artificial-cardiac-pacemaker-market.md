<p><p>The artificial cardiac pacemaker market encompasses several key types: standard pacemakers, implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICD), and biventricular ICDs. Standard pacemakers regulate heart rhythms by delivering electrical impulses. ICDs not only monitor but also treat life-threatening arrhythmias through defibrillation. Biventricular ICDs provide advanced therapy for heart failure patients by coordinating contractions in both heart ventricles. These technologies cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing treatment options and improving cardiovascular health outcomes in patients with arrhythmias and heart failure.</p></p>
