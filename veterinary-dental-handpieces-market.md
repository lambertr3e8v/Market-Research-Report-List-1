<p><strong>The Veterinary Dental Handpieces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Powered</li><li>Air Powered</li><li>Ultrasonic Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary dental handpieces market comprises three primary types: electric powered, air powered, and ultrasonic powered. Electric powered handpieces offer consistent torque and speed, enhancing precision in dental procedures. Air powered handpieces utilize compressed air for operation, providing versatility and lightweight design, but may have less torque than electric options. Ultrasonic powered handpieces use high-frequency vibrations for effective cleaning and scaling, ensuring gentle yet thorough dental care. Each type serves specific needs in veterinary dentistry, improving patient outcomes and clinician efficiency.</p></p>
