<p><p>The wind turbine friction material market includes three main types of brake pads: organic, sintered, and composite. Organic brake pads are made from natural fibers and resins, offering low wear but reduced durability under extreme conditions. Sintered brake pads, created through high-pressure processing of metal powders, provide excellent heat resistance and longevity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Composite brake pads combine various materials to optimize performance, delivering a balance of strength, durability, and noise reduction for wind turbine braking systems.</p></p>
