<p><ul><li>RCF</li><li>Low Bio-Persistent</li><li>Polycrystalline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic textile market comprises three main types: Reinforced Ceramic Fibers (RCF), Low Bio-Persistent fibers, and Polycrystalline fibers. RCFs are known for their high thermal resistance and strength, commonly used in industrial applications requiring heat insulation. Low Bio-Persistent fibers offer reduced health risks due to their slower degradation in biological systems, making them suitable for safer applications. Polycrystalline fibers provide excellent mechanical properties and thermal stability, often utilized in high-performance environments like aerospace and manufacturing, enhancing durability and efficiency.</p></p>
