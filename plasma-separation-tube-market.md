Glass tubes offer superior clarity and are preferred for high-precision testing, though they are more fragile. Composite tubes combine the strengths of both materials, providing durability and flexibility while maintaining a lower weight than glass. Each type serves specific needs in medical and laboratory settings, catering to varying preferences for safety and performance.</p></p>
