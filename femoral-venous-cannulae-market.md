<p><p>The femoral venous cannulae market is segmented into single-stage and two-stage types based on their design and functionality. Single-stage cannulae are characterized by a straightforward, single lumen that facilitates venous access for procedures like dialysis or medication delivery. In contrast, two-stage cannulae feature multiple lumens, allowing for simultaneous functions such as fluid administration and drainage, enhancing efficiency. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, influencing their adoption in various medical settings and impacting market growth.</p></p>
