<p><p>The Machine Room Less (MRL) passenger elevator market includes two primary types: traction passenger elevators and hydraulic passenger elevators. Traction elevators utilize a system of pulleys and ropes to lift the cab, offering energy efficiency and smooth operation, making them ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. In contrast, hydraulic elevators rely on fluid-driven pistons for movement, providing strong lifting capacity and is suitable for low-rise buildings. Both types cater to varied architectural needs while enhancing space utilization and performance.</p></p>
