<p><p>The Education Learning Management System (LMS) market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based LMS offers flexibility and scalability, allowing institutions to access learning materials and tools online without the need for extensive hardware. This model facilitates easy updates and collaboration. In contrast, on-premises LMS requires installation on local servers, providing control over data security and customization, but necessitating greater IT resources and maintenance. Both models cater to different needs and preferences in educational settings.</p></p>
