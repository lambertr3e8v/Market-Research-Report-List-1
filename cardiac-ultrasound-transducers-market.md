<p><p>The cardiac ultrasound transducers market includes various types designed for different imaging applications. Sector transducers offer a wide field of view for cardiac imaging. Microconvex transducers are compact and ideal for pediatric and adult cardiac assessments. Linear transducers provide high-resolution images for superficial structures. Pencil transducers, or phased-array transducers, focus on specific areas for detailed cardiac views. Concave transducers facilitate imaging of larger body areas. Other transducers encompass various specialized designs for unique diagnostic needs, contributing to the market's diversity.</p></p>
