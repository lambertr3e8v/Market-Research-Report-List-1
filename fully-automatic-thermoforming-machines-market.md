<p><p>The fully automatic thermoforming machines market comprises several types, including vacuum forming and pneumatic thermoforming, along with other methods. Vacuum forming utilizes a vacuum to mold heated plastic sheets over a solid form, creating detailed shapes with precision. Pneumatic thermoforming, on the other hand, employs pressurized air to shape the material, providing faster production rates and efficiency. Other thermoforming methods may include hybrid or tailor-made solutions that cater to specific industry demands, enhancing versatility in packaging and manufacturing applications.</p></p>
