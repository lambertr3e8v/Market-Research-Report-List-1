<p><p>Enterprise Payroll Outsourcing Solutions are categorized into two main types: Full-Managed Outsourcing and Co-Managed Outsourcing. Full-Managed Outsourcing involves transferring complete payroll responsibilities to an external provider who handles everything from processing payroll to compliance and reporting. In contrast, Co-Managed Outsourcing allows businesses to retain some payroll functions while collaborating with an external provider. This hybrid approach enables companies to maintain control over critical aspects while benefiting from the expertise and resources of the outsourcing partner.</p></p>
